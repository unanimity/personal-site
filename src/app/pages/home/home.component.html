Our approach

We build efficient and effective processes to achieve your goals.

  1. 1. Dedicated team

    Our company has a project-orientated structure. We offer dedicated teams for your projects to focus solely on your needs.

  2. 2. Flexible methodology

    We are aligned with your processes and tools. We select optimal methodology for your project: from iterative (Agile: SCRUM, Kanban) to predetermined (Waterfall). We work in your timezone.

  3. 3. Quick prototyping and deep analysis

    Requirements gathering, focus groups and usage analytics, user stories, quick prototyping, A/B testing, documented requirements and modern UX — we do everything to help you make reasonable and long-run decisions.

  4. 4. Quality assurance and control

    Defined test plans, test cases, test runs, quality metrics and reports. Quality management decreases cost and increases customer satisfaction.

  5. 5. Automated maintenance and reusability

    We reuse components, automate environments and delivery process, monitor and ensure 24x7 operations of your product.

  6. 6. Experienced staff

    We attract experienced professionals with interesting projects and modern technologies. We let them grow by offering trainings and supporting internal knowledge/lessons sharing.

  7. 7. Transparency

    Our work is completely transparent: detailed WBS with estimates and risks; predictable builds with release notes and known defects; who is working on what, everyone's performance indicators and detailed timesheet reports.
